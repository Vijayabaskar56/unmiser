# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-15_

Unmiser is a **local-first** expense tracker. This policy explains what the app
does and does not do with your data. In short: **your financial data never
leaves your phone.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Unmiser has no account, no sign-in, and no analytics or telemetry.
We do not operate a server that receives your data, and we cannot see anything
you do in the app.

## Data stored on your device

The following is stored locally on your device only, in the app's private
database:

- Bank SMS messages you grant the app permission to read, and the transactions
  parsed from them.
- Accounts, categories, budgets, subscriptions, and rules you create.
- App preferences (theme, accent, app-lock settings).

This data is never transmitted off the device by Unmiser. SMS parsing happens
entirely on-device.

## Permissions

- **SMS read** — used solely to detect and parse bank transaction messages on
  your device. Messages are processed locally; their contents are never
  uploaded.
- **Notifications** (optional) — used for local reminders only.
- **Biometrics / device passcode** (optional) — used only to unlock the app.

## Sharing transaction messages

When the app cannot recognize a message, it is kept locally so that _you_ can
choose to report it. Reporting is a one-tap action that pre-fills a GitHub
issue, which **you** review and submit yourself. Nothing is sent without that
explicit action.

## Data deletion

You can permanently erase all app data at any time from
**Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete all data**. Uninstalling the app also
removes all locally stored data.

## Changes

Because Unmiser is open source, any change to this policy is visible in the
project's commit history.
